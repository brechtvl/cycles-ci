Details

Each scene is rendered 5 times, with AA samples set for about 60s render time.

  • Master plots over time show the median render time, overlayed over the standard deviation.
  • Differential revisions show all runs, for the commits before and after the change. Times are plotted in % relative to the median of the before runs.
  • Local tags are plotted the same way as differential revisions, and compare two or more tags in a local git repository.
  • Click or hover data points to see more details for each.

GPU render times are very stable. However for CPU renders there is currently significant noise in the timings that makes it hard to draw conclusions besides overall trends.
